Return only valid JSON with this schema:
{
  "drug_name": "string | null",
  "strength": "string | null",

Field definitions:
- drug_name: the brand or generic name (e.g. "Tylenol", "Acetaminophen", "Children's Tylenol")
- strength: the amount of active ingredient per unit (e.g. "500mg", "325mg", "160mg/5mL", "25mg"). Do NOT include dosing instructions here.
- dose: the dosing instructions (e.g. "take 2 caplets every 6 hours", "1-2 tablets every 4-6 hours")
- form: the physical form (e.g. "tablet", "caplet", "capsule", "liquid", "suspension", "drops")

Rules:
- Use only the OCR text provided. Do not guess if uncertain.
- strength and dose are different: strength is per-unit concentration, dose is how much to take and how often.
- If the label says "500 mg" near the drug name, that is the strength.
- If the label says "take 2 caplets every 6 hours", that is the dose.
- For liquid forms, strength is often "Xmg/YmL" (e.g. "160mg/5mL").
- If multiple medications appear, choose the most likely primary product only if confidence is high.
- If no confident answer exists, use null values.
- Confidence must be between 0 and 1.
- Do not infer unsupported medical instructions.
